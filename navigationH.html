Dare Me

Application for people with the syndrome of down

This mobile application makes people with the syndrome of Down more independent. It's intented to let them function more independent in the daily life. The application is a game that dares the user to accomplisch assignments (educational, sporting, creative and communicative). They can collect different trophies by fulfilling the assignments. A fun and useful application.
